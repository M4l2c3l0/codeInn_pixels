Educational Websites

Where can I find the best online course?
Which one should I start with?
With all the available material online, there has never been a better moment to start learning about web development. The problem is that it is easy to become overwhelmed by it all, and we often just don't know where to start.
To assist you with your online quest, I have selected a few others than codeinnpixels that I have heard of, starting by the ones I personally used.

Codecademy

Price: Free*
Website: codecademy.com

Codecademy is an education company (...) We're committed to building the best learning experience inside and out, making Codecademy the best place for our team to learn, teach, and create the online learning experience of the future.
(source: codecademy.com)

*: Although it has a monthly fee for the Premium version, codecademy offers a very good learning experience and a whole lot of content for free (no trial period).
Unlike other websites using their free content as a subtle (or not so subtle) marketing tease, you have full access to all of its courses, which can be improuved by subscribing to the premium version.
Not just that, they also aim to assist beginners in finding their path, with all-round web development courses, both simple and interctive.
At the end of the day, all you need to get yourself started is an emal address, so there is really no reason not to give it a try.

Udemy

Price: N/A (by course)
Website: udemy.com

Udemy is a global marketplace for learning and teaching online where students are mastering new skills and achieving their goals by learning from an extensive library of over 40,000 courses taught by expert instructors.
(source: udemy.com)

As described above, Udemy is a marketplace where you basically go shopping for individual courses. No monthly fee here, as you only pay for the courses you want with a single one-off fee.
Anyone is given the chance to create and retail their own course(s), so although it allows unlikely teachers to shine and subjects to diversify, you are still advised as a student to look closely to a course's raitings before purchasing it.
On and all, I highly recommend anyone to have a look at it, as I truly believe that there are enough subjects and courses for all tasts and needs.

Lynda

Price: from $25 USD/month
Website: lynda.com

Lynda.com leading online learning platform that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals. Through individual, corporate, academic and government subscriptions, members have access to the lynda.com video library of engaging, top-quality courses taught by recognized industry experts.
(source: lynda.com)

For those of you who'd rather subscribe and pay a monthly fee to a more recognized and corporate educational institution, Lynda.com definitely is a good place for you to look at.
You are required to chose one of the available plans from basic to pro (or even corporate), and with the corresponding monthly fee, you will instantly be granted access to a very large and professional variety of content with courses developed by recognized teachers and/or experts in the domain.
